The New Season in Watamu is Here: The Kenyan Paradise Awaits!

June 06, 2025 Papa Remo Team News from Paradise

The wait is almost over! As summer unfolds, a new and exciting season is about to blossom on the coast of Kenya. Watamu, with its crystal-clear sea caressing white sandy beaches and its unspoiled nature, is ready to offer you an unforgettable experience.

A Jewel on the Indian Ocean

Picture yourself immersed in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes and the magical African atmosphere. This is Watamu, a jewel of Kenya that enchants travelers from all over the world every year. And in the beating heart of this paradise, there is a place that embodies its festive and relaxed soul: Papa Remo Beach.

The Ultimate Destination for Sun, Fun, and Flavour

Located in one of the most stunning bays, known as "Love Beach," Papa Remo Beach is the ultimate destination for those seeking not only sun and relaxation but also fun and unforgettable evenings. With the new season arriving, the staff is ready to reopen the doors to this corner of paradise, promising days of pure bliss and nights brought to life by beach parties under the stars.

  • Enjoy the mild and pleasant climate, perfect from July to October.
  • Explore the natural wonders of the Marine Park and Mida Creek.
  • Savour exquisite dishes at our renowned restaurant on the beach.
  • Join our legendary beach parties that light up the Watamu nights.

Pack your bags, Africa is calling! A summer you will never forget awaits you.
