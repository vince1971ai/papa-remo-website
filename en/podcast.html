About Our Podcast

The Papa Remo Podcast brings you stories, interviews, and insights from Watamu and the Kenyan coast. Hosted by our team, the podcast explores local culture, tourism, environmental conservation, and the unique experiences that make Watamu special.

Through conversations with local residents, business owners, conservationists, and visitors, we aim to share the rich tapestry of life on the Kenyan coast and highlight the beauty and challenges of this unique region.

Paparemo Beach Thematic

Voices of the Village

Interviews with the local community: historical fishermen, artisans, chefs, farmers, artists, and guardians of traditions who narrate the authentic soul of Watamu and its surroundings. For example: "The secrets of local ceramics with artisan Sofia."

Paparemo Through Your Eyes

Short, spontaneous interviews with tourists, both Italian and foreign, directly at Paparemo Beach. What impressed them? What was their experience? Fun anecdotes, like: "A family on vacation: the best moments at Paparemo."

Behind the Scenes at Paparemo

Interviews with the staff (lifeguards, bartenders, chefs, event organizers), stories about the preparation of special events, and Paparemo's philosophy of hospitality. For example: "How an evening event at Paparemo is born."

Hidden Treasures & Itineraries

Dedicated to discovering the surrounding area: small villages, nature trails, lesser-known food and wine specialties, and popular traditions. An episode idea: "Excursion to the abandoned lighthouse: history and legend."

Sustainable Paparemo

A focus on Paparemo Beach's sustainability initiatives, interviews with environmental experts on coastal protection, and tips for more conscious tourism. Positioning Paparemo as an environmentally aware actor.
