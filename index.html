About Papa Remo Beach

Nestled along the pristine shores of Watamu, Papa Remo Beach offers a unique blend of Italian charm and Kenyan hospitality. Our beachfront location provides breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean and direct access to one of Kenya's most beautiful beaches.

Whether you're joining us for a meal at our Italian restaurant, celebrating a special occasion, or staying in our exclusive village, we aim to create memorable experiences that capture the magic of coastal Kenya.

Founded with a passion for authentic cuisine and a love for Watamu's natural beauty, Papa Remo Beach has become a beloved destination for locals and international visitors alike.
