Beachfront Living

Papa Remo Village offers exclusive beachfront properties in one of Kenya's most beautiful coastal locations. Our collection of luxury villas and apartments combine Italian design with local craftsmanship, creating unique spaces that blend seamlessly with the natural environment.

Whether you're looking for a holiday home, a permanent residence, or an investment property, Papa Remo Village provides an opportunity to own a piece of paradise on the Kenyan coast.

All properties feature stunning ocean views, private access to the beach, and are within walking distance to Papa Remo Beach Restaurant and other local amenities.

Build Your Dream Home

We offer a limited number of prime beachfront and near-beach plots for those who wish to design and build their own custom home in Papa Remo Village.

Plot sizes range from 500 sqm to 2000 sqm, with various locations available. Our team can connect you with trusted local architects and builders who understand the local environment and building regulations.

All plots come with necessary permits and access to village amenities and infrastructure.
