Beach Weddings

Say "I do" with the Indian Ocean as your backdrop at Papa Remo Beach

Your Dream Beach Wedding

Papa Remo Beach offers a stunning natural setting for your wedding day. With the white sands of Watamu beach, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and swaying palm trees, our venue provides the perfect backdrop for your special day.

Our experienced wedding planning team will work closely with you to create a personalized celebration that reflects your vision and style. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we can accommodate weddings of various sizes and styles.

With our Italian-Kenyan fusion cuisine, beautiful accommodation options, and attention to detail, your wedding at Papa Remo Beach will be an unforgettable experience for you and your guests.
