Our Restaurant

Experience authentic Italian cuisine with a Kenyan twist, overlooking the beautiful Indian Ocean

Taste of Italy in Kenya

Papa Remo Beach Restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Italy to the shores of Watamu. Our Italian chef creates dishes that blend traditional recipes with the freshest local ingredients, resulting in a unique culinary experience.

Enjoy your meal with breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean, feeling the sea breeze while savoring our homemade pasta, wood-fired pizzas, and the catch of the day prepared with Mediterranean flair.

Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, our attentive staff will ensure your dining experience is memorable.

Menu Highlights

Homemade Pasta

Our pasta is made fresh daily using traditional Italian methods. Try our signature seafood linguine with locally caught prawns, calamari, and mussels in a light tomato sauce.

Fresh Seafood

Our seafood is sourced daily from local fishermen. Enjoy grilled fish of the day, lobster thermidor, or our popular mixed seafood platter, all prepared with Mediterranean herbs and spices.

Our restaurant offers a unique dining experience with tables set directly on the beach, allowing you to feel the sand between your toes while enjoying your meal.
