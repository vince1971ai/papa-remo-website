Our Podcast Series

Welcome to "Sounds of Watamu," a podcast series that captures the essence of life at Papa Remo Beach. Through these audio experiences, we invite you to immerse yourself in the natural sounds, music, and stories that make our corner of Kenya so special.

From the gentle lapping of waves on the shore to interviews with local community members, our podcasts offer a unique way to connect with Watamu even when you're far away.

Listen to our episodes below, and let the sounds transport you to our beautiful beach.
