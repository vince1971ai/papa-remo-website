Paparemo Village

Your home away from home in Watamu

A LUXURY RESIDENTIAL COMPLEX ON THE SEVEN ISLANDS BEACH IN WATAMU.

Paparemo Village offers exclusive beachfront properties in one of Kenya's most beautiful coastal locations. Our collection of luxury villas and apartments combine Italian design with local craftsmanship, creating unique spaces that blend seamlessly with the natural environment.

Whether you're looking for a holiday home, a permanent residence, or an investment property, Papa Remo Village provides an opportunity to own a piece of paradise on the Kenyan coast. All properties feature stunning ocean views, private access to the beach, and are within walking distance to Papa Remo Beach Restaurant and other local amenities.

Activities & Experiences

During your stay at Paparemo Village, you can enjoy a variety of activities and experiences:

  • Snorkeling and diving in the Watamu Marine National Park
  • Deep-sea fishing excursions
  • Dolphin watching trips
  • Visits to the Gede Ruins and Arabuko-Sokoke Forest
  • Sunset dhow cruises on Mida Creek
  • Kitesurfing and other water sports
  • Cultural visits to local villages

Our concierge can help arrange any of these activities and provide recommendations based on your interests.
