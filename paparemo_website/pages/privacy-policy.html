Personal Information We Collect

We collect various types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

When you fill out the contact/reservation form on our Site, you provide us with certain personal data necessary to respond to your request. This data may include:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To allow you to interact with third-party features on our site, such as the podcast player and chatbot.

Your Rights
