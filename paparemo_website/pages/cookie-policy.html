What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that sites visited by users send to their terminals, where they are stored to be re-transmitted to the same sites on the next visit. This site uses different types of cookies.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure the proper functioning of the site, to improve your Browse experience, and to collect aggregate information about the number of users and how they visit the site. Storing your preference for the dark or light theme, for example, uses a similar technology (localStorage) to function.

Types of Cookies We Use

Technical and Functional Cookies

These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of the site and to provide the requested features. They do not require your consent.

  • User Preferences: We use the browser's `localStorage` technology (similar to a cookie) to store your choice between the light and dark theme and apply it to your future visits.
  • Vercel Hosting: Our site is hosted on Vercel, which may use technical cookies for security and traffic distribution.

Third-Party Cookies

While Browse the site, you may also receive cookies from different sites or web servers (so-called "third-party cookies") on your device. This happens because the site may contain elements such as images, maps, sounds, or specific links to web pages of other domains that reside on servers other than the one on which the requested page is located. These cookies are set directly by website operators or servers other than our Site.

Specifically, our site integrates the following services:

  • Spreaker: To embed our podcast players. Spreaker may install cookies for the player's functionality and for its own analytics. For more information: Spreaker Privacy Policy.
  • Elfsight: To embed the events calendar. Elfsight may use cookies to ensure the widget's functionality. For more information: Elfsight Privacy Policy.
  • Zapier: For the functionality of the chatbot. This service may install cookies to manage the chat session. For more information: Zapier Privacy Policy.

Profiling and Marketing Cookies

This site does not directly use profiling cookies to send advertising messages in line with the preferences expressed by the user during their web Browse. However, the third-party services listed above may collect data that falls into this category, according to their respective policies.

How to Manage and Disable Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences directly within your browser, preventing third parties from installing them. Through your browser preferences, it is also possible to delete cookies installed in the past. It is important to note that by disabling all cookies, the functionality of this site may be compromised.

You can find information on how to manage cookies in your browser at the following addresses:
