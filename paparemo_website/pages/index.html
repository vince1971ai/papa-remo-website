About Papa Remo Beach

Established in 1997 by Remo Lenzi, Papa Remo Beach Bar has become an iconic destination on the Kenyan coast. Located on the beautiful Jacaranda Road in Watamu, our beach bar offers a perfect blend of relaxation, dining, and entertainment.

Whether you're looking to enjoy a delicious meal with ocean views, host a special event, or simply unwind with a cocktail on the beach, Papa Remo Beach Bar provides the perfect setting.
