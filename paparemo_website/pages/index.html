About Papa Remo Beach

Established by the visionary Remo Lenzi back in 1997, Papa Remo Beach Bar swiftly became a cherished name. It stands today as a truly iconic destination, a jewel nestled on the beautiful Kenyan coast.

You will discover our haven on Watamu's picturesque Jacaranda Road, a location second to none, directly overlooking the famed and enchanting "love beach." Our venue proudly offers a unique and captivating blend of serene relaxation for peaceful days, delightful dining experiences with ocean vistas, and vibrant, engaging entertainment under the stars.

The cuisine is primarily, and authentically, Italian at its heart, featuring the freshest local seafood, classic wood-fired pizzas, and delicious pasta.
