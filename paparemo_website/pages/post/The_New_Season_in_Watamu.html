A Luxury Residential Complex in the Heart of Watamu

Paparemo Village is an exclusive residential complex offering a blend of luxury, comfort, and the natural beauty of the Kenyan coast. Located just a short walk from the pristine sands of Papa Remo Beach, the village provides a serene and secure environment for your perfect getaway or long-term residence.

Our accommodations are designed with a unique blend of local charm and modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable and memorable stay. Whether you're seeking a peaceful retreat or a base for exploring the wonders of Watamu, Paparemo Village is your ideal home away from home.
