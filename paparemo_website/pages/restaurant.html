Dining at Papa Remo

Our restaurant offers a unique dining experience that combines delicious cuisine with breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. Located on the beautiful Watamu beach, Papa Remo Restaurant is the perfect place to enjoy a meal while taking in the natural beauty of the Kenyan coast.

We pride ourselves on serving fresh, locally-sourced ingredients prepared with a blend of Italian and Kenyan influences. Our menu features a variety of seafood dishes, traditional Italian favorites, and local specialties, ensuring there's something for everyone to enjoy.
