Dining at Papa Remo

Our restaurant offers a unique dining experience that combines delicious cuisine with breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean. Located on the beautiful Watamu beach, Papa Remo Restaurant is the perfect place to enjoy a meal while taking in the natural beauty of the Kenyan coast.

We pride ourselves on serving fresh, locally-sourced ingredients prepared with a blend of Italian and Kenyan influences.

The Papa Remo Experience

Lunch by the Sea

Enjoy a relaxed lunch with your feet in the sand. Our daytime menu offers light, fresh options perfect for a sunny day on the Kenyan coast. The atmosphere is casual and welcoming for families and beachgoers.

Dinner Under the Stars

As the sun sets, Papa Remo transforms into a magical dining spot. Experience a romantic dinner with the sound of the waves, or a vibrant evening with friends, enjoying our exquisite dinner menu and curated wine list.
