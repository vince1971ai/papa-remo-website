Dining at Papa Remo

Our restaurant offers a unique culinary experience that combines delicious cuisine with a breathtaking view of the Indian Ocean. Located on the beautiful Watamu beach, Papa Remo Restaurant is the perfect place to enjoy a meal while admiring the natural beauty of the Kenyan coast.

We pride ourselves on serving fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, prepared with a blend of Italian and Kenyan influences. Our menu offers a variety of seafood dishes, classic Italian favorites, and local specialties, ensuring a wide choice for all tastes.

Sunset Aperitivo

Experience the magic of the Italian aperitivo at Papa Remo. As the sun sets over the Indian Ocean, relax with one of our signature cocktails, a glass of fine wine, or your favorite drink. It's the perfect moment to unwind and soak in the breathtaking views from our beachfront location.

To make the experience even more special, every aperitivo is accompanied by a selection of delicious complimentary appetizers, freshly prepared by our chefs to tantalize your palate before dinner.
