The Story of Papa Remo Beach

Founded by the visionary Remo Lenzi in 1997, Papa Remo Beach quickly became a beloved household name. Today, it stands as an iconic destination, a cherished gem nestled on the stunning Kenyan coast.

You'll discover our paradise on the picturesque Jacaranda Road in Watamu, a unique location overlooking the renowned and enchanting "love beach". Our venue offers a unique and captivating blend of relaxation for lazy days, delightful ocean-view dining experiences, and vibrant, engaging entertainment under the stars.
