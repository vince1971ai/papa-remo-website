The Story of Papa Remo Beach

Founded by the visionary Remo Lenzi in 1997, Papa Remo Beach quickly became a beloved name. Today, it stands as an iconic destination, a jewel nestled on the stunning Kenyan coast.

You'll discover our paradise on the picturesque Jacaranda Road in Watamu, a unique location overlooking the famous and enchanting "beach of love." Our venue offers a unique and captivating blend of relaxation for quiet days, delightful culinary experiences with an ocean view, and vibrant, engaging entertainment under the stars.

The cuisine is primarily and authentically Italian, featuring the freshest local seafood, classic wood-fired pizzas, and delicious pastas.
